Frequently Asked Questions

Practical answers about our work, technical focus, and collaborations.

What is Bladio Studio?

Bladio Studio is a Luxembourg-based technical studio building custom software, real-time systems, and interactive applications. We focus on reliability, structure, and systems that work under real conditions.

Do you take on client work or commissions?

Yes — selectively. We work on external projects when they involve clear technical requirements, custom software, real-time logic, or system-level challenges.

What types of projects are a good fit?

Good fits include live event systems, internal tools, CLI utilities, custom software, simulations, and technically driven interactive applications.

What do you not do?

We do not build generic marketing websites, template-based agency projects, or one-off gimmicks without long-term technical value.

What technologies do you work with?

Depending on the project: C++, C#, JavaScript/TypeScript, Unreal Engine, Unity, Node.js, Python, and custom system architectures. Tools are chosen based on requirements — not trends.

Are you hiring or accepting collaborators?

We are not actively hiring. However, we are open to hearing from technically strong developers or designers for future collaborations.

How can we contact you?

Use the contact page or email us directly at contact@bladiostudio.com.
